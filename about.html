About Me

I’m Celesta

UX/UI designer. Artist. Art Director. Dancer

I come from a very arts heavy background. I’m also very keen on technology, organization, and design. I have found that UX design allows me to combine and demonstrate all of my previous training and skill.

Due to my arts background and playful imagination, my work is slightly unorthodox which leads to an innovative and distinctive design.

UX Design

Leading with my user-centered mindset , paired with my ability to problem solve and open mind when collaborating with clients, I have found that I excel in user research.

UI and Development

A dedication to detail, persistence, and thoroughness has proven itself exceptionally useful in UI. I thrive on the, what some may consider, tedious tasks such as redlining, prototyping, and developing.

Art Direction

Being in various arts fields, I have found myself in leadership roles on many occasions. A strength is being able to visual and execute a project from concept to execution.
